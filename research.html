Working Paper
Monetary Policy Transmission Mechanism for Bangladesh: An ARDL Approach
Examines the channels through which monetary policy decisions propagate across the Bangladesh economy, using the Autoregressive Distributed Lag (ARDL) & Nonlinear Autoregressive Distributed Lag (NARDL) modelling framework on time-series data.
→
03
Working Paper
Social Perception, Career Choice and Labour Market Outcomes in the Digital Gaming Industry: Evidence from Bangladesh
Investigates how social attitudes shape career decisions in Bangladesh's emerging digital gaming sector and the consequent effects on labour market participation and earnings.
